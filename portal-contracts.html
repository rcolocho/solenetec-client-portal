1. Scope of work
Solenetec LLC agrees to furnish all materials, labor, equipment, and supervision necessary to complete the installation of a residential solar photovoltaic system and battery storage at the property described above, in accordance with applicable building codes and manufacturer specifications.

2. Contract price & payment
Total contract price: $42,600. Payment is due in four installments tied to project milestones: deposit (20%), permit approval (30%), installation complete (26.5%), and PTO & project close (23.5%). Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month.
3. Warranty
Enphase provides a 25-year product warranty on all microinverters and a 10-year warranty on battery storage units. Solenetec provides a 10-year labor warranty covering workmanship defects. Roof penetration warranty: 10 years.
4. Permit & interconnection
Solenetec is responsible for obtaining all required building permits and managing the SDG&E interconnection application on behalf of the client. Estimated permit timeline: 3–6 weeks from contract execution.
